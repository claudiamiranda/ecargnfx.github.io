Involve Teachers, Innovate Learning.

EdTech Experts

We value entrepreneurs who have personal experience pain points in learning that technology can solve. We expect each of our cohort companies to contain at least one teacher by trade or training. We’re subject-agnostic (cooking is as valid as chemistry), but we believe the best solutions come from firsthand experience of problems.

Abundant Resources

EdTech Experts

We offer seed funding, collaborative workspace, free legal and PEO services, brand identity, design coaches, engineering and programming mentors, hosting and storage, media and public relations, relationships with domain-specific investors, and more. We’re entrepreneurs ourselves, so our offer terms convey our respect for our cohort companies. We all succeed by growing great businesses that effect meaningful change in education.



Technical Interoperability,

EdTech Experts

We’re committed to reducing barriers to adoption by striving for interoperability among cohort technologies. We partner with leading learning and ed tech networks to provide cohort companies access to user groups for customer development and user acquisition. We’re also building an internal collaborative sales network: our faculty will leverage their knowledge and networks to help our companies grow sales efficiently, and alumni are lifetime members. Technical interoperability helps maximize the benefits of our beta channels and collaborative sales network.

Longer Cohorts

EdTech Experts

Each cohort will consist of four months of accelerator curriculum with two additional months of shared workspace for a total of six months to better accommodate the longer maturation cycles of learning tech companies. After Commencement Day, alumni companies are encouraged to rent adjacent coworking space at discounted rates so they can continue to benefit from our resources and contribute to future cohorts.
